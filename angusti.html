ANGUSTI

€50

ANGUSTI is our perfume with a carefully constructed formula that is ideal for when you are planning your moment of relaxation either in the morning or in the afternoon. With the subtle smell of lavender, your body is instantly reminded to slow down and relax. The rich essential oils we derive from lavender moisturize your skin and create a soft smell. It lifts the mood and lowers blood pressure, ideal when you are planning you #allmetime during the day. We recommend using this perfume for activities such as going for a walk, reading a book or meditating.

Within the Netherlands, if your order is placed on business days before 22:00 pm, it will be shipped the same day. You can expect your order to be delivered the next business day. If you order on a Saturday or Sunday, we ask you to be a little more patient: you can expect your order the following Tuesday. For orders shipped to a country outside the Netherlands, delivery may take slightly longer. We offer free shipping on all orders over €100 within the Netherlands and Belgium. For orders of less than this, delivery costs are calculated on the checkout page. The standard shipping rates are:

• The Netherlands: €6,50
• Belgium: €7,50
• France: €8,95

When your package leaves the warehouse, we send an email notifying you that your package is on its way, along with a track & trace link to track your package.

For this fragrance we are using the lavender plant Lavandula Angustifolia, which our farmers organically grow in the east of Spain.

How to use it:
We recommend spraying a maximum of three times. You can put the fragrance on different places on your body like on your wrists, behind your ears and on your neck.
